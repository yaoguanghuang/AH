Screenshot of Independent Game The Adventurer. Embeded in March 2025.

I have been a passionate gamer since childhood. In my teens years, I used to view games as my asylum separating from reality as well. However, games are far more than that—it simulates diverse experiences in the most accessible way, while this magic circle repeatedly fosters reflection, empathy, reconciliation, and even reinterpreting.

Now as an independent game designer, I enjoy the process of testing mechanics and eventually building them together into playable prototypes, as well as mapping out concepts and narrations to form a world with its specific features and stories. I often incorporate social issues or personal narratives into my designs and embedded them into the stories. I hope my games not only entertain, but also encourage players to think, feel, and experience different perspectives of the world and of life itself.

Anyway, please enjoy this Arcade Gallery of mine!
