(P1-50)I Am Fine Thank You and You?(50P). Laser printed on bond paper(5.625*7.5 Inches) in May 2025.

I Am Fine Thank You and You? is a short comics (1-4 pages) collection that specifically indexed comics exploring the feelings and stories of Psychological disabilities and disorders. Considering the entertaining and simplified nature of comics, I tried to incoprate this serious social topic with the sense of black humor, irony and exaggerated into each of my narration in panels.

Most of stories are drawn from my friends, as well as from my own life and experiences as an artist living with Generalized Anxiety Disorder. They also serve as visually exaggerated interpretations of the emotions and decisions associated with such disorder-related experiences.

Updates to be expected (Last Updates 10/28/2025).
