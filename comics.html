COMICS

I mainly working on illustrating the short comics exploring the theme of Psychological Disabilities and Mental Disorder, which is expect to be all indexed in my comic zine/book I Am Fine Thank You And You? Considering the entertaining and simplified nature of comics, I tried to incoprate this serious social topic with the sense of black humor, irony and exaggerated into each of my narration in 1 to 4 pages.

Most of the stories come from personal life/experience as an artist with general Anxeity Disorder, or serve as visually exaggerated interpretations of emotions and decisions associated with various disorder-related experiences.
