Infinite is a Platformer Game where you will play the role of a clawless sparrow who is designed to spend its whole life relentlessly flying until the end. During the playthrough of each level, the 3 different phrases of sparrow’s life expand and reveal the reason and meaning of its journey.

LEVEL1

Level 1 Map. Designed and illustrated in May 2024.

L1 represents the first phase of the clawless sparrow’s life: the early childhood of General Z Chinese students (2008-2013), while they experienced an economic boom and the late phase of the one-child policy. The game art of this map tries to replicate the town scene of the millennial years of China, the memory of my childhood about streets and architecture. L1 is relatively easy to finish because the player owns the complete HD screen as the sparrow flying space(1920*1080).

Level 1 Sparrow Design. Designed and illustrated in March 2024.

Level 1 Sparrow State. Designed and illustrated in March 2024.

The sparrow for this level is a fledgling with lighter, cleaner colors as well, who also owns plenty of energy that is easy to recover. In this stage, the player will feel that the sparrow is energetic, light-weighted, and easy to control, just like the times-benefits of the economic boom experienced in childhood.

LEVEL2

Level 2 Map. Designed and illustrated in June 2024.

L2 represents the second phase of the clawless sparrow’s life: teen years of General Z Chinese students(2013-2018), while they experienced the benefits of national growth and acceptance and propaganda of striving culture. The game art of this map tries to replicate the city scene of the economic peak of China, the memory of my teen years about the new tech, advertisements, and thriving industries. L2 starts to have the space squeeze compared to the L1, the flying space becomes narrower(1920*945).

Level 2 Sparrow Design. Designed and illustrated in April 2024.

Level 2 Sparrow State. Designed and illustrated in April 2024.

The sparrow in this level is a teenager with medium color, who owns less energy, and takes more time to recover energy than the first stage, however, it is faster, actually the fastest. In this stage, the player will feel like the sparrow is less energetic, in a rush, and probably harder to control.
