Welcome to achieveofaurown!

This is Aurora Huang, an independent game designer focusing on experimental games with immersive mechanics and compelling narratives. So far, I have developed and published seven indie game demos across various genres, all available in the Games section. My work explores how mechanics, narrative, and aesthetics interact to humanistically simulate diverse perspectives and unique experiences, which generates not only entertainment, but also reflection, reinterpretation, empathy, and even socially transformative values.

Meanwhile, I am also a comic illustrator who published a short comic collection titled I Am Fine, Thank You and You, printed in two editions using both laser and risograph techniques. In addition, I am a trained visual artist skilled in typography, book and page design, and UI/UX design.

I hope my all players/audience find as much joy in experiencing my works as I do in creating them.
