AH

Aurora Huang is a game designer, comic illustrator and visual communication artist. She is mainly working on experimental independent games that applys story-narrative, artist aesthetic and critical thinking.

Currently, her creative interest focus on serious social topics such as Gender Equality and Women's Rights, Psychological Disorders and Treatments, and History from Below VS History from Above. She often brings up specific social issues from real-life experiences, recreating them by up building gameplay structures and scenes in unexpected perspective. Except Concepting and Core Gameplay Structure, she considers Game narration to be the most significant part of Game Design.
